## The Brief

The lot fell off sharply behind the house — useful living space on the upper level off the kitchen, useful shade space on the lower level for summer afternoons. The original wood deck used neither well, and it was past saving.

## What We Built

A double-tier composite structure with Trex Transcend boards on both levels, hidden-fastener system throughout, and a wraparound aluminum railing that keeps the sightlines open across both terraces. Stairs run between the levels at an angle that turns the descent into part of the room rather than just a connector.

## Details

- Trex Transcend composite boards, full hidden-fastener system
- Aluminum cable railing on both levels for unobstructed sightlines
- Structural rebuild down to the footings (the originals weren't code)
- Integrated low-voltage post and stair lighting
- Built in 14 working days; permitted and HOA-approved
