## The Brief

A single-level ranch with sliding doors off the great room and nothing useful beyond them. The owners hosted often and wanted the outdoor space to behave like another room of the house — flush threshold, deep enough to seat ten, clear sightlines to the mountains.

## What We Built

A wide ground-level composite deck stepped down half an inch from the sliders so the indoor and outdoor finishes read as one continuous floor. We pulled the boards in a single direction across the long axis to push the eye toward the view, and ran a low cable railing only where code required it.

## Details

- TimberTech Advanced PVC boards (handle freeze-thaw with zero board movement)
- Picture-framed perimeter detail
- Low-profile aluminum cable railing
- Concealed footing system to keep the under-deck profile clean from the lawn
- Built in 9 working days
