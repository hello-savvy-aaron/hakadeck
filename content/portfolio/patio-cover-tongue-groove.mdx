## The Brief

An open patio off the back of the house got too much sun to be usable in summer. The goal was a permanent cover that felt like an extension of the home rather than a bolt-on.

## What We Built

A gabled patio cover with a warm tongue-and-groove wood ceiling, carried on stained timber posts and tied into the existing roofline. Recessed lights and a fan make the space work into the evening.

## Details

- Tongue-and-groove wood ceiling with a stained finish
- Stained timber posts on the existing patio slab
- Recessed lighting and a ceiling fan
- Tied into the home's existing roofline
