## The Brief

A tall walk-out elevation had room for two separate outdoor spaces stacked above each other — an upper deck off the main floor and a balcony below — connected by a single run of stairs.

## What We Built

A two-tier elevated structure on substantial cedar posts, with black metal railing on both levels and an exterior staircase linking them down to the yard. The stacked design doubles the usable outdoor space on a narrow footprint.

## Details

- Two stacked deck levels on cedar support posts
- Black metal railing throughout
- Exterior staircase connecting both levels to grade
