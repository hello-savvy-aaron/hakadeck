## The Brief

The homeowners wanted shade over an existing concrete patio and a way to screen the grill and cooking gear from the dining area without closing the space in.

## What We Built

A solid patio cover on stone-wrapped columns that ties into the home's masonry, paired with a freestanding horizontal-slat privacy screen. The screen hides the griddle and grill while still letting light and air through to the seating area.

## Details

- Patio cover on stone-veneer columns matched to the house
- Horizontal-slat privacy screen for the grill station
- Built over the existing patio slab
