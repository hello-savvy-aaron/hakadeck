## The Brief

The homeowners wanted privacy for the patio grill area and the hot tub without building a solid fence — keep the light, the air, and the views of the yard beyond.

## What We Built

Freestanding horizontal-slat privacy screens in wood-tone composite on black metal posts — a straight run backing the grill station, and an L-shaped section wrapping the hot tub. The slat spacing breaks up sightlines from the surrounding yards while still letting light and breeze through.

## Details

- Wood-tone composite slats on black metal posts
- L-shaped screen wall sheltering the hot tub
- Post-cap lights on the grill-side run
- Anchored to the existing patio slab
