## The Brief

A shaded backyard under tall pines needed a private spot for a hot tub plus a low deck that could carry the spa load and connect it to the existing patio.

## What We Built

A composite deck sized for the hot tub with horizontal-slat privacy panels wrapping the spa, screening it from the neighbors while keeping the tree-shaded feel. The low profile keeps the step-in easy and ties cleanly into the surrounding hardscape.

## Details

- Composite deck engineered for the hot tub's load
- Horizontal-slat privacy screen around the spa
- Low-profile platform for an easy step-in
- Set into the existing tree-shaded patio
