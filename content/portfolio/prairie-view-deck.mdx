## The Brief

A new build on the edge of open prairie called for an elevated deck that made the most of the long views, with a staircase down to grade and a sheltered patio tucked beneath.

## What We Built

A two-story composite deck on stained timber posts, wrapped in black aluminum cable railing that all but disappears against the horizon. A switchback staircase steps down to the yard, and the framing overhead creates a covered lower patio off the walk-out level.

## Details

- Composite decking on both levels with a full hidden-fastener system
- Black aluminum cable railing for uninterrupted prairie and mountain views
- Switchback staircase from the upper deck down to grade
- Covered lower patio off the walk-out basement
